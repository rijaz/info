Welcome!

Who Am I?

I am currently a junior studying Computer Science at Boston University with an expected graduation date of May 2018. Looking for any internship opportunity to gain experience, work with awesome people and learn some more awesome coding skills!

I was introduced to Computer Science when I was a biology major through Python my freshman. I fell in love with Computer Science through the class' assignments - our final project was to write a simple version of a program that would compare texts to reveal if it was written by the same author!

I later learned Java, C, Haskell, SQL and R and taught myself CSS and HTML!

I'm currently working on three class projects:

1. A web application as part of a team called Couch Potato using Django/Python. Couch Potato would allow users to search a movie and be given a list of viewing options based on their current subscriptions to viewing sites such as Netflix, Hulu, etc. instead of having to individually check all the sites

2. A final project for my Introduction to Databases and Data Mining class that requires a team of 3 people to mine data from a site and represent the data in a presentation. Topic and specifics TBD!

3. An android app as part of a team in Global App Initiative for Freedom House . The app would send reminders to users about the colleges that they hope to apply to and would connect them to advisors via a chat.
